WAWM Shining Strings

Who are we?

The WAWM Shining Strings are an advanced group of string players. The musicians in this group range from 6th grade, all the way through 12th grade.

The Shining Strings group plays gigs and concerts in the West Allis and Milwaukee area. Last year, in the 2016/2017 season, we played at a tea party and at a middle school concert.

What type of music/what instruments do we play?

The sections of our orchestra are 1st violin (melody), 2nd violin (harmoney/backup), viola, cello, and bass. We play a wide variety of music, from pop, to classics, to rock, the Shining Strings group has something for everyone. A shining strings classic is the Lady Gaga Hit Mix arranged by Larry Moore. It is a mash-up of three of Lady Gaga's biggest hits, Poker Face, Bad Romance, and Just Dance. We play this particular song at every single gig we do.
